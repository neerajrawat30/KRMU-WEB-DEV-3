Paneer Butter Masala

Prep Time: 15 mins | Cook Time: 25 mins | Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer cubes
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 3 tbsp fresh cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil and 1 tbsp butter in a pan. Saute onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  4. Mix in turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, and salt.
  5. Add paneer cubes and 1/2 cup water. Simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  6. Stir in cream and remaining butter. Cook for 2 more minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.

Tip: Soak paneer in warm water for 10 minutes before cooking to keep it soft.
